Mount Madja-as

A breathtaking view of the Madja-as mountain range. The perfect spot for hiking enthusiasts looking for a challenge with panoramic views.

Difficulty Level: High

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.9)

Trail Duration: 2 to 3 days for experienced hikers, 4 to 5 days for beginners

Distance: Flores Trail: Approximately 24.8 km (12.4 km ascent and 12.4 km descent) Panipiason Trail: Approximately 46.6 km (23.3 km one-way)

Best Time to Visit: November to February (Dry season)

Elevation: 2,117 meters (6,946 feet) above sea level

Perfect for experienced hikers looking for a challenging yet rewarding experience. The hike offers stunning views at the summit, perfect for photography enthusiasts.
