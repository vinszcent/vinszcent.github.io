Mt. Igcoron

Scenic and rewarding, Mt. Igcoron offers a rich hiking experience with panoramic views of surrounding valleys and mountains.

Difficulty Level: Moderate

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8)

Trail Duration: Approximately 2 to 3 hours to reach the summit, depending on your pace and weather conditions.

Distance: The trail covers an estimated distance of about 3 to 4 kilometers one-way.

Best Time to Visit: December to March (dry season) for clearer trails and better visibility.

Elevation: Approximately 570 meters (1,870 feet) above sea level.

Great for nature lovers and those seeking a short but fulfilling climb through diverse terrain.
