Mt. Igmatongtong

Majestic view over Mt. Igmatongtong's rugged peaks. Perfect for those who want a challenging but rewarding hike.

Difficulty Level: Medium

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.6)

Trail Duration: Approximately 1 to 2 hours to reach the summit, depending on your pace and weather conditions.

Distance: The trail covers a distance of about 2 to 3 kilometers one-way.

Best Time to Visit: December to March (dry season) for clearer trails and better visibility.

Elevation:Approximately 800 meters (2,624 feet) above sea level.

For intermediate hikers, Mt. Igmatongtong offers an unforgettable experience with spectacular vistas at the summit.
